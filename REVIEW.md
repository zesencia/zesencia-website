## 5. SEO & Accessibility

### SEO Observations
- Likely low indexation footprint
- Weak semantic structure (H1/H2 hierarchy unclear from available data)
- Limited authoritative backlinks

### Accessibility Issues (Assumed)
- No visible WCAG compliance signals
- Missing alt-text discipline likely
- Potential contrast and navigation accessibility gaps

### Recommendations
- Implement:
  - Proper heading hierarchy (H1 → H2 → H3)
  - Meta titles + descriptions per page
  - Schema.org structured data (Organization, Product)
  - WCAG 2.1 AA compliance baseline
